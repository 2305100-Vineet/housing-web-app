## 📋 Step-by-Step Implementation

### Step 1 — Vendor Master Creation `XK01`
The Vendor Master is the foundation of all procurement in SAP MM. Stores General Data (name, address, bank details), Company Code Data (reconciliation account, payment terms), and Purchasing Org Data (currency, incoterms). Mandatory before any PO can be created.

---

### Step 2 — Material Master Creation `MM01`
Stores all product data across views — Basic Data (description, UoM), Purchasing View (order unit, tolerances), MRP View (lead time, procurement type), and Accounting View (valuation class, price control). The valuation class drives automatic GL account posting during GR and MIRO.

---

### Step 3 — Purchase Requisition `ME51N`
Internal procurement request with no financial or legal binding. Captures plant, material, quantity, delivery date, and cost center. Auto-generated by MRP runs (MD01/MD02) or created manually. Converts directly into a PO once approved.

---

### Step 4 — Purchase Order `ME21N`
Legally binding commercial document issued to the vendor (document type: **NB Standard PO**). Captures vendor, material, quantity, price, delivery schedule, payment terms, and incoterms. Triggers **commitment accounting** in CO — reserving budget before physical receipt.

---

### Step 5 — Goods Receipt `MIGO` *(Movement Type 101)*
System confirmation of physical material receipt. Simultaneously:
- **MM:** Stock increases, open PO quantity reduces
- **FI:** Auto-posts `Debit Inventory` / `Credit GR/IR Clearing`

Creates linked Material Document + Accounting Document for permanent audit trail. Enforces three-way match at receipt stage.

---

### Step 6 — Invoice Verification `MIRO`
Three-way match: Invoice vs PO vs GR. If within tolerance:
- `Debit GR/IR Clearing` *(clears temporary liability)*
- `Credit Vendor Account` *(creates actual AP liability)*

Invoices exceeding tolerance are **auto-blocked** for payment pending review.

---

### Step 7 — Payment Processing `F-53` / `F110`
Final step — settles vendor's open AP liability.
- **F-53 (Manual):** `Debit Vendor` / `Credit Bank Clearing` — clears open item
- **F110 (Automatic):** Batch-processes all due invoices, applies early payment discounts, generates DME bank transfer files

> 🔗 Complete document chain: **PR → PO → GR → MIRO → Payment** — permanently linked and auditable in SAP.

## ⭐ Unique Features

### Business Value
- ✅ End-to-end automation eliminates manual handoffs across procurement, warehouse, and finance
- ✅ Three-way match (PO–GR–Invoice) prevents overpayment and fraudulent invoicing
- ✅ Automated payment terms management captures early payment discounts
- ✅ Real-time stock updates at GR eliminate inventory inaccuracies
- ✅ Complete document chain ensures full audit readiness and regulatory compliance

### Technical Highlights
- ⚙️ GR/IR clearing account mechanism auto-bridges goods receipt and invoice receipt
- ⚙️ Tolerance-based invoice blocking enforces pricing discipline without human intervention
- ⚙️ Moving Average Price (MAP) valuation keeps inventory at real-time cost
- ⚙️ F110 handles multi-currency, high-volume payment batches with bank file generation
- ⚙️ Commitment accounting in CO provides real-time budget monitoring from PO creation

## 📊 Financial Accounting Flow

```
GOODS RECEIPT (MIGO)
    Debit  → Inventory Account         (Stock increases)
    Credit → GR/IR Clearing Account    (Temporary liability)

INVOICE VERIFICATION (MIRO)
    Debit  → GR/IR Clearing Account    (Clears temporary liability)
    Credit → Vendor Account            (Actual AP liability created)

PAYMENT (F-53 / F110)
    Debit  → Vendor Account            (Clears AP liability)
    Credit → Bank Clearing Account     (Payment made)
```
